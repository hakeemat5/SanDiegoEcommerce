Shipping Information

Delivery Options

Standard Shipping

Our standard shipping option delivers your African groceries and products within 3-5 business days. We carefully package all items to ensure they arrive in perfect condition.

  • Orders under $50 $7.99
  • Orders $50 - $100 $5.99
  • Orders over $100 FREE

Express Shipping

Need your African groceries quickly? Our express shipping option delivers your order within 1-2 business days.

  • All orders $14.99

Local Delivery (San Diego Area)

We offer same-day or next-day delivery for customers in the San Diego area. Place your order before 12 PM for same-day delivery (when available).

  • Orders under $75 $9.99
  • Orders over $75 FREE

Order Processing

Orders are typically processed within 24 hours of being placed. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped.

Perishable Items

For perishable African food items, we use insulated packaging and ice packs when necessary to ensure freshness. We recommend being available to receive these deliveries to maintain quality.

Shipping Delays

While we strive to deliver all orders on time, occasional delays may occur due to weather conditions, carrier issues, or other unforeseen circumstances. We will notify you of any significant delays affecting your order.

Questions about shipping?

If you have any questions about our shipping policies or need assistance with tracking your order, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team.
