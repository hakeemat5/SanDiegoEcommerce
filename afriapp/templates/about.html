Our Journey

About Our Nigerian Store

Founded in 2018, Nigerian Treasures was born from a passion to bring authentic Nigerian products to the diverse community of San Diego. What started as a small family business has grown into a beloved cultural hub where both Nigerian expatriates and curious enthusiasts can discover the rich traditions and craftsmanship of Nigeria.

Our store specializes in high-quality, hard-to-find Nigerian products that showcase the country's incredible diversity. From vibrant Ankara fabrics and handcrafted art to aromatic spices and traditional foods, we carefully source each product directly from Nigerian artisans and producers to ensure authenticity and excellence.

Our Nigerian Cultural Mission

At Nigerian Treasures, we believe our products are more than just merchandise—they're powerful connections to heritage and identity. For many in our Nigerian-American community, finding authentic products means being able to maintain cultural traditions and celebrate their identity thousands of miles from home.

We take pride in serving as cultural ambassadors, introducing the vibrant culture of Nigeria to curious Americans in San Diego. Through our products, cultural demonstrations, and community events, we celebrate Nigeria's rich heritage spanning diverse ethnic groups including Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa, and many others.

Our store features products from all regions of Nigeria, showcasing the country's incredible diversity. From Lagos to Abuja, Kano to Port Harcourt, Calabar to Enugu—we bring together treasures that represent Nigeria's cultural mosaic, with each item telling a unique story of craftsmanship and tradition.

Beyond just selling products, we're committed to education and community building. We regularly host cultural workshops, fashion shows, and food tastings that bring people together to learn about and celebrate Nigerian traditions in an authentic and respectful way. Our goal is to create a bridge between Nigerian culture and American appreciation.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Authenticity is at the heart of everything we do. We work directly with Nigerian artisans, craftspeople, and producers to ensure that every product in our store represents the true traditions and quality craftsmanship of Nigeria. We believe in preserving cultural heritage while making it accessible to all, creating economic opportunities for Nigerian creators.

Community is central to our mission. We've created a welcoming space where Nigerian expatriates can find a piece of home and where curious Americans can discover the beauty of Nigerian culture. Our store serves as a gathering place where connections are made through shared appreciation of Nigeria's rich cultural traditions.
